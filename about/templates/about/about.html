About Us

Align Pilates was founded by us, Leah and Catherine in July 2019 right before all of our worlds were turned upside down by the global pandemic. We decided to set up this studio as we both have learned through personal and professional experience the value of movement and how it can transform not only our physical health but our mental health also.
Both Catherine and Leah have taken part in Polestar Pilates 1000 hour training and are now very lucky to both be Polestar instructors after completing their many many certifications in Pilates.
Our ethos in the studio is to move as much as you like while also to move as gently as you need to on any given day. We want our cleints to get as much as they can from this studio whether it be an intense workout or a mellow movement class.
We welcome all in our studio. Our belief in Pilates is so strong that we think there isn't a person in the world who wouldn't benefit from Pilates.
Our community at Glow is ever growing and we always open for new members. If our studio is somewhere you would be interested in joining please register here.
