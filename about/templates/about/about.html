About Us

Align Pilates was founded by us, Leah and Catherine in July 2019 right before all of our worlds were turned upside down by the global pandemic. We decided to set up this studio as we both have learned through personal and professional experience the value of movement and how it can transform not only our physical health but our mental health also. Our ethos in the studio is to move as much as you like while also to move as gently as you need to on any given day. We want our cleints to get as much as they can from this studio whether it be an intense workout or a mellow movement class.
